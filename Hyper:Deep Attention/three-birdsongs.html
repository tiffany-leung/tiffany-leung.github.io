three birdsongs

by philip pacey

hover down to read full poem

1

so many birds as wryneck quite

quwrrr twirl their rrr's:

and some lisp as the nightingale her s's

in sweet thweet thweet.

2

oak in garden copse in flower.

song of the willow-wren.

Wickers nest , have you found?

giggling and wickering

3

the nightingale has two songs

he says sirrap, sirrap,

and then he says tweet.

(from richard jefferies' notebooks.

april 15 1878, april 15 1882, june 3 1879)

girl waves
