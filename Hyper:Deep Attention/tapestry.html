tapestry

by paul brown

hover down to read full poem

Ignorance;

blind old woman

Shaping the Formless;

potter making pots

Self-Awareness;

monkey

Distinction Between Self and Non Self;

boatman and ferry

Sense Perception and Understanding

empty house

Contact;

embarrassing couple

Feeling (Pain);

arrow piercing mans eye

Desire For Worldy Enjoyment;

drinking wine

Indulgence;

eating fruit

Continuity of Existence;

woman in bed

Birth;

woman in childbirth

Death;

man carrying bundle containing a corpse

(from a buddhist tapestry, horniman museum

london)
