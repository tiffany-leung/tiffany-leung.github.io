The first time I came to this restaurant was for a Bumble networking event. I find this place so beautiful and funny enough, is the exact same image I picture a perfect brunch place with my friends during the weekends. The food was great, not just because it was free but also because they had innovative bite-sized pasta served in little plastic bowls.
