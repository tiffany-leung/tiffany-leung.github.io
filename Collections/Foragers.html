Foragers is the convenient supermarket right around the corner when I needed to get last minute groceries. Just a few minutes away from where I stay, I usually go there to get an emergency avocado, piping-hot ready made food whenever I am too lazy to cook a meal or get boujee "healthier" snacks when I want something to munch on. The prices of the products here may not be the most ideal (they can sell up to $10 for a bar of chocolate), but I really just go there when I want a healthier food option, without having to walk too far away from my living accomodations.

Foragers Market Foragers is nestled in the neighbourhood of Chelsea, on 8th Avenue and 22nd Street. Besides being an upscale gorcery store, it is also a cafe (for coffee and muffins) and also a restaurant for full meals. Despite its upscale pricing, it IS in Chelsea, so there has to be a place where people can get their muffins and food made from organic produce.
